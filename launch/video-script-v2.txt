DELIVERY NOTES

- Don't sell. State facts. Problem is real, solution is yours, here's the link.
- The personal-proof section is the heart. Don't rush. Each accomplishment is a beat.
- Pause before "One folder." That's the reveal. Let it land.
- End flat. No "what are you waiting for?" energy. Confident, calm.

────────────────────────────────────────────────

RECORDING NOTES

- Format: 9:16 vertical for X/LinkedIn/TikTok native. Re-cut to 16:9 for YouTube + landing page hero.
- No royalty-free background music. Single ambient pad track or silence with sharp edits. Music makes it feel like a SaaS ad, which kills trust.
- No captions burned in for the launch cut. Add captions in post.
- Shoot ratio: record each section 3-4 times. The HOOK alone deserves 10+ takes.
